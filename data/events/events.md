### The Castle Ward Shakedown

**Event Information**
* **Session:** Session 1
* **Location:** Castle Ward, Waterdeep

**Description**
While traveling to the Yawning Portal, the party was intercepted by a City Watch patrol. A corrupt sergeant (referred to in transcripts as Sergeant Tatra or Vontra) extorted the group for a "disruption by implication fee" or "expeditionary fee" to allow them to pass without being held up by bureaucratic paperwork. This established the current atmosphere of opportunistic corruption within the city’s lower-ranking officials.

### The Yawning Portal Brawl

**Event Information**
* **Session:** Session 1
* **Location:** The Yawning Portal

**Description**
A violent altercation broke out in the middle of the tavern involving a group of thugs. During the chaos, a massive troll emerged from the central well (the entrance to Undermountain), accompanied by several stirges. The party assisted the tavern's proprietor, Durnan, in dispatching the creatures, marking their first public display of combat prowess in Waterdeep.

***

### The Dock Ward Warehouse Raid

**Event Information**
* **Session:** Session 1
* **Location:** A Warehouse on Candle Lane

**Description**
Following a lead on the disappearance of Floon Blagmaar, the party infiltrated a Zhentarim-controlled warehouse. They discovered the aftermath of a skirmish between rival gangs (Zhentarim and Xanathar Guild). Inside, they recovered several dossiers and documents addressed "For the Master," providing the first concrete evidence of a larger shadow war occurring within the city.

***

### The Sewer Hideout Clash

**Event Information**
* **Session:** Session 2
* **Location:** Waterdeep Sewers / Xanathar Guild Hideout

**Description**
The party tracked kidnappers into the city’s sewer system, leading to a confrontation within a secret Xanathar Guild outpost. The event culminated in a battle against a "Gazer" (a small beholder-kin) and a half-orc named Grum'shar. This event revealed that the Xanathar Guild was actively using the sewer network to move prisoners and contraband beneath the streets of the city.

***

### The_Acquisition of Trollskull Manor

**Event Information**
* **Session:** Session 3
* **Location:** Castle Ward / North Ward

**Description**
As a reward for their services and because he lacked the promised gold, Volothamp Geddarm legally transferred the deed of Trollskull Manor to the party. This transition moved the group from being transient mercenaries to property owners within the North Ward, though the manor was noted to be in significant disrepair and rumored to be haunted.

***

### The_Discovery of the Golden Hand

**Event Information**
* **Session:** Session 4
* **Location:** Various

**Description**
Through investigation of recovered documents, the term "Golden Hand" surfaced as a recurring symbol or faction name. This discovery shifted the party's focus from simple rescue missions to a broader investigation into a mastermind or organization that appears to be manipulating both the Zhentarim and the Xanathar Guild from the shadows.

### The Field Ward Refugee Crisis

**Event Information**
* **Session:** Session 0.5 / Session 1
* **Location:** The Field Ward (North of the City)

**Description**
The "Field Ward" was established as a massive, unofficial tent city housing thousands of refugees displaced by the Tyranny of Dragons. The sessions highlighted the dire conditions there—cold, mud, and lack of resources—and the City Watch’s refusal to provide protection to those living outside the main walls, leading to the rise of local "spokespeople" and vigilante efforts to maintain order.
