**Location Information**
* **Name:** Waterdeep (The City of Splendors)
* **Region:** The Sword Coast, North of the Sea of Swords.
* **First Met:** Session 0.5 (Refugee camps/Field Ward) / Session 1 (City proper).

**Description**
* **The Metropolis:** A massive, walled city-state and the most influential hub of commerce and intrigue on the Sword Coast. It is divided into several distinct wards (Castle, Dock, North, Sea, Trades, and Southern Wards), each with its own socioeconomic atmosphere.
* **The Government:** Currently ruled by Open Lord Laeral Silverhand, following the ousting of the corrupt Dagult Neverember. The city is protected by the City Watch (police) and the City Guard (military), and is famously overseen by the secret "Masked Lords."
* **Current Climate:** The city is still reeling from the aftermath of the Tyranny of Dragons, with a high influx of refugees living in the "Field Ward"—a muddy tent city located between the inner and outer north walls.
