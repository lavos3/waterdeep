**Location Information**
* **Name:** Trollskull Manor
* **Ward:** North Ward (Trollskull Alley)
* **First Met:** Session 3 (Acquisition)

**Description**
* **The Property:** A four-story manor house that formerly served as a tavern. It features a large taproom on the ground floor, a kitchen, a cellar, and multiple floors of living quarters above.
* **Condition:** Upon acquisition, the building is in a state of neglect, requiring significant renovation and gold investment to return it to a functional business or residence.
* **History:** The manor was gifted to the party by Volothamp Geddarm as payment for rescuing Floon Blagmaar. Local rumors suggest the building is haunted by the spirit of a former barman named Lif.
