**Location Information**
* **Name:** The Yawning Portal
* **Ward:** Castle Ward
* **First Met:** Session 1

**Description**
* **The Inn:** A world-famous tavern known primarily for the 40-foot-wide stone well in the center of its common room. This well serves as the primary entrance to Undermountain, a massive subterranean dungeon.
* **Atmosphere:** The tavern is stone-built with a slate roof, featuring multiple balconies looking down into the common room. It is a hub for adventurers, rumor-mongers, and those looking to hire specialized talent.
* **The Proprietor:** Managed by Durnan, a man of few words and a veteran adventurer who once returned from Undermountain with a legendary fortune.
