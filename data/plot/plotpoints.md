### **Waterdeep: Shadows over Waterdeep – Campaign Plot Points**

#### **Session 1: The Yawning Portal & The Warehouse**
* **The Call to Action**: The party received an urgent *Sending* from Volothamp "Volo" Geddarm, requesting their immediate presence at the Yawning Portal.
* **The Mission**: Volo tasked the party with finding his friend, Floon Blagmaar, who went missing after a night of drinking at the Skewered Dragon.
* **The Zhentarim Warehouse**: The party tracked Floon’s trail to a warehouse in the Dock Ward. Inside, they engaged in combat with members of the Zhentarim (The Black Network) and discovered several corpses, indicating a violent turf war with the Xanathar Guild.
* **The Rescue of Renaer**: Instead of Floon, the party rescued Renaer Neverember, the son of the former Open Lord of Waterdeep. Renaer was being held by the Zhentarim for information regarding his father's "hidden hoard."

#### **Session 2 & 3: Into the Sewers**
* **Tracking the Kidnappers**: Following information obtained at the warehouse, the party entered the city's sewer system to track the Xanathar Guild, who had intercepted Floon.
* **The Xanathar Hideout**: The party successfully infiltrated a secret hideout. During the confrontation, they witnessed an envoy of Xanathar (a Mind Flayer) escaping through a portal.
* **Combat with the Devourer**: The party defeated a Mind Flayer's Intellect Devourer and the remaining guild members guarding the location.
* **Saving Floon**: The party successfully rescued a badly beaten Floon Blagmaar and escorted both him and Renaer back to the surface.

#### **Session 4: Trollskull Manor & New Foundations**
* **The Reward**: Unable to pay the promised gold, Volo instead gifted the party the deed to Trollskull Manor, a dilapidated and purportedly haunted estate in the North Ward.
* **The Haunted Tavern**: The party explored their new property, discovering its history as a former tavern and encountering the resident ghost, Lif the barkeep.
* **Faction Interest**: The party's heroics began to draw the attention of major city factions, including the City Guard and various secret societies within Waterdeep.

#### **Session 5: The Embassy & The Master's Gifts**
* **The Fent Embassy**: The party arrived at the Fent Embassy to follow up on leads regarding the "Golden Hand" and the escalating gang war.
* **The Meeting with Lord M**: The party met with the enigmatic Lord M, who provided them with their first significant equipment upgrades—including specialized darkness arrows—to aid in their investigation.
* **The Emerging Threat**: The party began to piece together a larger conspiracy involving entities known as "The Enigma" and "The Wraith," suggesting the gang violence is a cover for a deeper, more coordinated threat to the city.
