**Character Information**
* **Name:** Mirt (The Moneylender)
* **First Met:** Referenced in historical dossiers and party notes (Session 1-3 context)

**Description**
* **Physical Appearance:** A large, jovial-looking man, often appearing as a wealthy, somewhat eccentric merchant or moneylender.
* **Personality:** A retired adventurer and a "Masked Lord" of Waterdeep. He is a vital advisor to the city's leadership and a member of the Harpers.
* **Role:** He is a legendary figure in Waterdeep’s history, known as "Mirt the Merciless" in his younger days, now serving as a primary point of contact for high-level city intrigue.
