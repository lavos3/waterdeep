**Character Information**
* **Name:** Volothamp "Volo" Geddarm
* **First Met:** Session 1 (at the Yawning Portal)

**Description**
* **Physical Appearance:** Traditionally known as a flamboyant and eccentric figure, though when the party first encountered him, he appeared uncharacteristically "tired, ragged, and bloodshot."
* **Personality:** A famous traveler and author known for his high energy and occasional tendency to promise rewards that outweigh his current liquid assets. He is currently anxious regarding the disappearance of his friend, Floon Blagmaar.
* **Role:** An old friend of Madrugal who provided the party's first major quest in Waterdeep.
