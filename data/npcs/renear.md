**Character Information**
* **Name:** Renaer Neverember
* **First Met:** Session 1 (rescued from a warehouse in the Dock Ward)

**Description**
* **Physical Appearance:** A handsome man of noble bearing. When first met, he was found bound and gagged in a warehouse, having been kidnapped by the Zhentarim.
* **Lineage:** He is the son of Dagult Neverember, the former Open Lord of Waterdeep and current Lord of Neverwinter.
* **Reputation:** Despite his father’s reputation for corruption, Renaer is known to be a man of much higher character who has distanced himself from his father's politics.
