**Character Information**
* **Name:** Floon Blagmaar
* **First Met:** Mentioned in Session 1; tracked/found in Session 2 (Xanathar Guild Hideout)

**Description**
* **Physical Appearance:** A remarkably handsome human male with wavy hair, often noted for his striking resemblance to Renaer Neverember (which led to his accidental kidnapping).
* **Personality:** Described as a socialite who enjoys the nightlife of Waterdeep but lacks the combat prowess or political connections of his companions.
* **Status:** He is a close friend of Volo and was the primary objective of the party's initial rescue mission in the sewers.
