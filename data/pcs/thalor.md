### Thalor.md

**Character Information**
* **Name:** Thalor Caladras
* **Player:** Mehdi
* **Class:** Ranger
* **Subclass:** Gloomstalker

**Description**
* **Physical Appearance:** A tall, lean High Elf with weathered features. He has golden hair, typically tied back for practicality, and sharp, alert green eyes.
* **Distinguishing Marks:** Pale scar tissue from black dragon acid burns is visible on the back of his neck and collar.
* **Attire & Equipment:** He wears well-maintained studded leather armor under a deep green ranger’s cloak. He carries a longbow and a scimitar worn smooth from use. He wears a small, dark wooden wolf pendant that he frequently handles.

**Backstory**
* **Military Service:** Thalor served in an elven ranger battalion during the Tyranny of Dragons.
* **The Northern Betrayal:** During the northern assault at the Well of Dragons, his unit expected reinforcements that never arrived. The battalion was nearly wiped out, leaving Thalor as one of only a handful of survivors.
* **The Survivor's Guilt:** He and his companions (Madrugal and Imiren) bonded in a recovery camp after the war, initially bickering over the military failures before realizing they were searching for the same truths.
* **Current Life in Waterdeep:** Seeking solitude, he lives in a cabin just outside the city walls. He makes a living as a hunter and trapper, selling pelts and meat to the city's butchers.
