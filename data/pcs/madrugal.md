**Character Information**
* **Name:** Madrugal
* **Player:** Will
* **Class:** Cleric
* **Subclass:** Life Domain
* **Deity:** Lathander (The Morninglord)

**Description**
* **Physical Appearance:** A 37-year-old human male with tan skin that remains consistent year-round. He has tousled honey-brown hair and matching golden/honey-brown eyes.
* **Attire & Holy Symbol:** He wears a washed-out marigold yellow undershirt beneath layered, heavy brown wool clerical vestments. His holy symbol is a hollow golden circle with radiating sun points, described as "dirty gold" due to its use throughout the war.
* **Personality:** A veteran war medic with a "gallows humor" developed from the front lines. He is wise and peaceful but maintains a realistic, grounded perspective on the costs of battle.

**Backstory**
* **Military Service:** Madrugal served as a mercenary medic during the Battle at the Well of Dragons (1490 DR) as part of the southern assault.
* **The Southern Collapse:** His unit was thrown into chaos after their leader went missing; his company was destroyed in the ensuing battle, leaving him as the sole survivor.
* **Current Life in Waterdeep:** He lives a low-standard life in Waterdeep to prioritize helping others. He funds his mission by working as a private doctor for wealthy nobles, using that gold to provide medical aid and "Sacred Flame" warmth to refugees and orphans in the Field Ward.
